Values

Our research group values the following four equally important principles:

  • Excellence in research: We are committed to conducting rigorous, original, and impactful research that advances scientific knowledge and contributes meaningfully to society while maintaining the highest ethical standards.
  • Supportive environment: We believe in supporting each other through mentorship, celebrating successes, and fostering a diverse and inclusive environment where everyone has the opportunity to grow and thrive.
  • Collaboration: We foster an environment of open collaboration and active knowledge sharing among team members and external collaborators.
  • Societal impact: We strive to ensure our research outcomes benefit society and address real-world challenges.
